🍽️ Meal Mission

“Turning Surplus Food into Shared Hope.”

📖 Introduction

Meal Mission is a social impact platform designed to address two major global challenges: food waste and hunger.

Every day, restaurants, hotels, and event organizers discard large amounts of edible food, while many individuals struggle to find their next meal. Meal Mission bridges this gap by connecting food donors with non-governmental organizations (NGOs) that distribute food to people in need.

The platform ensures that surplus food is redistributed efficiently instead of being wasted.

🎯 Project Objective

The main objectives of Meal Mission are:

Reduce food wastage

Help provide meals to hungry and underprivileged people

Create a systematic platform for food redistribution

Encourage social responsibility among food providers

🚨 Problem Statement

Food wastage and hunger exist simultaneously across many parts of the world.

Key Issues

Restaurants, hotels, and events generate large amounts of surplus food

Millions of people still suffer from hunger and food insecurity

There is no direct system connecting food donors with NGOs

As a result, edible food is often thrown away instead of reaching people who need it.

💡 Proposed Solution

Meal Mission provides a digital platform that connects:

Food Providers (Hotels, Restaurants, Events)

NGOs that distribute food to the needy.

Food providers simply update the availability of surplus food on the website, and the system connects them with nearby NGOs that can collect and distribute it.

This approach ensures that good food reaches people instead of being wasted.

⚙️ How the System Works
1️⃣ Food Availability Update

Restaurants, hotels, or event organizers post surplus food details including:

Name of the provider

Location

Quantity of food available

2️⃣ NGO Matching

The platform identifies nearby NGOs that are registered in the system.

3️⃣ Food Collection

The NGO receives the request and collects the food from the provider.

4️⃣ Food Distribution

The NGO distributes the collected food among needy and underprivileged communities.

🔔 Recent Enhancement
Real-Time NGO Notification System

To improve efficiency and response time, Meal Mission now includes a real-time notification system for NGOs.

Whenever surplus food is listed on the website:

The system immediately detects the new food listing.

Nearby registered NGOs receive instant notifications.

The notification includes:

Provider Name

Food Location

Quantity of Food

NGOs can respond quickly and arrange pickup.

Benefits

Faster food pickup

Reduced chances of food spoilage

Better coordination between donors and NGOs

Faster food distribution to needy people

This enhancement ensures surplus food reaches people as quickly as possible.
